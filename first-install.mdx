## Intro

Thank you for buying Sunscreen, it means the world to me! Such a huge plugin can feel overwhelming very quickly, so below is a short summary of what to do after you've just bought a license.

### Setup

The first step is, of course, to drag the `Sunscreen.jar` file into your `/plugins` folder and restart your server. Sunscreen does not depend on any other plugins.

After logging in you'll be greeted by a welcome message with some further instructions, in case you missed it, the key points are as following:

1. Sunscreen will generate its unique resource pack for your server once, it never changes after the initial generation.
2. Nexo, ItemsAdder and CraftEngine are supported out of the box, after the resource pack has been generated it'll automatically put itself into your chosen plugin and reload. Sunscreen might prompt you to allow it to turn off some features for a seamless integration.
3. The resource pack will automatically be merged into BetterHud and MythicHud, and the installed plugin of choice will reload.
4. Opening the editor will prompt you with a (skippable) walkthrough of the features.
